Pre-School for your child

At EuroKids, our endeavour is to provide your child a comfortable atmosphere where learning is full of fun. The Pre-School is your child’s first experience away from you and it’s a huge leap for you as a parent. Hence, we ensure a safe, hygienic and engaging environment for your child. Learning at EuroKids happens through experience and reflection on doing, the integrated use of scientifically designed games, toys and technology makes learning a lot more fun and engaging.

Our curriculum goes beyond reading, writing and counting numbers. A major part of your child’s day would include singing, dancing and exploring play activities encouraging them to learn, grow & discover at their own pace. The curriculum is developed on the basis of research findings by the 'Centre on The Developing Child at Harvard University' that focuses on building Executive Function Skills (EFS) in your child.

EUROKIDS ADVANTAGES

Speech, Pronunciation, Expression, Application and Knowledge is a phonic based language program which helps your child learn language with emphasis on pronunciation, diction and proficiency.

EuroKids Buddy is a parent app to provide updates about their child on a real-time basis like pictures of school events, activities and celebrations. It will be equipped with a daily diary and will also enable the swift exchange of notifications and messages from EuroKids to the parents and vice-versa.

Our EuroMusic curriculum aims to provide age-appropriate musical experiences that helps your child discover and develop an ear for music.

EuroGym is India’s first specialized children’s gym that provides quality fitness training to children. It includes a healthy dose of running, jumping and movement to keep your child active.

This unique program uses technology and digital aids to provide curriculum in the form of interactive animated games, stories and videos that enhance learning abilities within children.

A toy library, which gives your child access to select international learning toys. These toys are age appropriate and enable multiple skill development in your child.
